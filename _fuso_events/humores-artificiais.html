The film touches on themes such as the relationship between artificial intelligence and humanization, the anthropology of humor and laughter, scientific knowledge and ancestral wisdom, nature and technology, capitalism and contemporary society, artificial consciousness, and human emotion while discussing post-colonial, gender and identity issues. imagem: /assets/img/gabriel-abrantes_still-2.jpg credito_imagem: © Coleção Museu de Serralves layout: event ---
