As obras resultantes deste encontro do realizador com as paisagens e as pessoas desta ilha oferecem um novo olhar e um novo discurso que enriquecem o património artístico dos Açores. \ **\ Daniel Blaufuks foi o primeiro convidado a realizar um filme que explorasse a paisagem e a atmosfera açorianas. Filmando ao som das centenas de pequenas nascentes e ribeiras do Parque Terra Nostra, Blaufuks fez And all will be repeated, que teve estreia mundial no FUSO INSULAR em 2020.** texto_curatorial_eng: >- FUSO INSULAR Independent Film Screenings Independent cinema is a category that highlights a filmmaker’s personal artistic vision and expression.  One of its distinguishing features is the respect for the freedom of the Diretor. FUSO INSULAR is committed to promoting independent cinema by inviting experienced and renowned Diretors to shoot on the island of São Miguel, resulting in films that reflect their personalities and singular views of the world around them. \ **\ Daniel Blaufuks was the first artist invited to explore the landscape and the atmosphere of the Azores. To the sound of hundreds of small springs and streams in the Terra Nostra Park, Blaufuks made And all will be repeated, which had its world premiere at FUSO INSULAR in 2020.** imagem: /assets/img/arqu_logo_screen_s_pos.png link_vimeo: NO VIMEO layout: programa ---
