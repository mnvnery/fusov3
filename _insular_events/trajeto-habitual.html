Inevitavelmente, um corpo que gira sobre si próprio desequilibra-se e, muitas vezes, acaba por cair, mostrando a sua fragilidade e falta de controlo. It delves into the unpredictability of our existence, highlighting what Alberto Caeiro referred to as "the astonishing reality of things" in contrast to their impermanence and ephemerality. The performance portrays a body in constant motion, reminiscent of a carefree child exploring its boundaries, all within the same fluid movement. It evokes the feeling of dizziness and the resulting shift in perception while enduring as a method for comprehending reality and conveying emotional expression. Inevitably, a body spinning upon itself becomes unbalanced and often ends up falling, revealing its fragility and lack of control.
